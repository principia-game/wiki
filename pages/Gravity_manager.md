Manages the global gravity. Use **`IN0`** and **`IN1`** to set the gravity based on angle and force. Modify the gravity multiplier with **`IN2`**.

## Socket information
- **`IN0`**: Angle
- **`IN1`**: Force
- **`IN2`**: Active (+ modifier)
